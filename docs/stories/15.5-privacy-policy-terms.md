# Story 15.5: Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

## Status: InProgress

## Story

**As a** game publisher,
**I want** proper legal pages in place,
**so that** we comply with App Store requirements and build user trust.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Privacy Policy page accessible at `/privacy`
2. Terms of Service page accessible at `/terms`
3. Pages are static HTML, no JS required
4. Links accessible from main menu or footer
5. Content covers data practices accurately
6. GDPR-compliant disclosures
7. iOS App Store requirements met

## Offline Work Tasks

These tasks require NO internet - just writing and local testing.

## Dev Notes

### App Store Requirements
Apple requires:
1. Privacy Policy URL in App Store Connect
2. Privacy "nutrition label" accurately filled out
3. Disclosure of any tracking (ATT framework if applicable)

### GDPR Requirements
For EU users:
1. Clear consent for data collection
2. Easy way to request data deletion
3. Data portability option
4. Clear explanation of data use

### CCPA Requirements
For California users:
1. "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" (if applicable)
2. Right to know what data is collected
3. Right to delete personal information

### Legal Disclaimer
**These templates are starting points, not legal advice.** Consider having a lawyer review before publishing, especially for:
- EU market (GDPR)
- California market (CCPA)
- Children's content (COPPA)
