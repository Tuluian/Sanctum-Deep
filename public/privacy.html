Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 5, 2025

Welcome to Sanctum Ruins ("the Game"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our roguelike deck-builder game.

Quick Summary: We collect minimal data, store your game progress locally on your device, and never sell your information. If you create an optional account, we store only what's needed for cloud saves.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information (Optional): If you choose to create an account for cloud saves, we collect your email address and/or Google account identifier.
  • Purchase Information: When you make in-app purchases, payment processing is handled by Apple (iOS), Steam, or our payment processor. We do not store credit card information.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Game Progress: Your save data, unlocked content, achievements, and settings are stored locally on your device. If you create an account, this data is optionally synced to our servers.
  • Anonymous Analytics (Optional): We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data to improve the game (e.g., which classes are most popular, average run length). This data cannot identify you personally.
  • Error Reports: If the game crashes, anonymous error information may be sent to help us fix bugs.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your precise location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites
  • We do not use advertising trackers

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To save and restore your game progress
  • To sync your progress across devices (if you opt in with an account)
  • To process purchases and unlock premium content
  • To respond to support requests
  • To improve the game based on anonymous usage patterns
  • To fix bugs and improve stability

3. Data Storage & Security

Your game progress is primarily stored locally on your device using standard browser storage (localStorage). If you create an account, a backup is stored on our secure servers hosted by Heroku. We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For iOS app distribution and in-app purchases (Apple's privacy policy applies)
  • Steam: For desktop distribution and achievements (Valve's privacy policy applies)
  • Google Sign-In: For optional account authentication (Google's privacy policy applies)
  • Heroku: For cloud save hosting (Salesforce's privacy policy applies)
  • Sentry: For anonymous error tracking (Sentry's privacy policy applies)

These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

5. Children's Privacy

Sanctum Ruins is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information from your child, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your save data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Opt-out: Disable cloud sync and play locally only

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

For EU/EEA Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

For California Users (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected and request its deletion. We do not sell personal information.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

Local save data on your device is retained until you clear it or uninstall the game.

8. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. If you access the Game from outside the US, your data may be transferred to and processed in the US. By using the Game, you consent to this transfer.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the Game or by email (if you have an account). The "Effective Date" at the top indicates when the policy was last updated.
